HandSketch — pinch to draw

Allow camera. Pinch (index + thumb) to draw. Try gestures below.
Brush: 6 px
Smoothing: 0.6
Gestures
• Pinch (thumb+index) → draw (hold).
• Pinch + middle finger close → Eraser mode.
• Open palm (all fingers straight) held 1s → Clear canvas.
• Pinch + quick left/right swipe → change color (cycle).
• Pinch pressure (pinch tighter) → thicker stroke (simulated).
Tip: mirror on — the app flips the camera so drawing feels natural.
